About me.

My mother was born here in Melbourne and my father was born in Solerino, Sicily and migrated from Italy when he was just 3 years old. I have lived, worked and studied in Melbourne all my life.

At the end of 2016 I decided to leave my job and study IT Networking at Holmesglen Chadstone. I started in 2017 and studied part time, finishing at the end of 2018.

I now work nights, organising my partners accounts for his current business and am studying Introduction to IT and Introduction to Programming at RMIT University, online. Next year I hope to study a Bachelor Degree in IT at RMIT.
