I looked into jobs at Google and selected the role of a Software Engineer. I think this would be a fascinating job to have because Google is responsible for developing a wide range of technologies and software. The role of a Software Engineer at Google is fascinating and varied. In this job, you would be part of a team working on a specific software solution for one of Googles many innovative products. This sounds very appealing to me because I think it would give a great experience to work with other people and learn so many different things about many different ground breaking technologies that I may never get the chance to interact with anywhere else.
